First, and this is the lead story, Innovent Biologics announced that I B I three forty-three — generic name arcotatug tavatecan — met its primary endpoints in the international phase three G-HOPE study in previously treated advanced gastric and gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma. The compound is a claudin eighteen point two antibody-drug conjugate carrying an exatecan topoisomerase one inhibitor payload, with Fc silencing to reduce off-tumor effects, conjugated via cleavable linker at drug-to-antibody ratio of four. Both progression-free survival and overall survival hit; the company called the safety profile tolerable with low gastrointestinal toxicity. The National Medical Products Administration in China has accepted the new drug application under priority review, making this the first claudin eighteen point two antibody-drug conjugate anywhere in the world to enter regulatory review. Takeda holds exclusive rights outside Greater China through the October twenty twenty-five global strategic partnership — one point two billion dollars upfront including a hundred million-dollar equity investment at premium, up to ten point two billion dollars in milestones, royalties. Second, ADC Therapeutics had a brutal Wednesday. The phase three LOTIS-five confirmatory trial of Zynlonta — generic name loncastuximab tesirine, a C D nineteen-directed antibody-drug conjugate — in combination with rituximab for relapsed-refractory diffuse large B-cell lymphoma hit the primary endpoint of progression-free survival, with no detrimental effect on overall survival. However, twenty-seven deaths, or thirteen point two percent, were recorded in the treatment arm versus nine, or four point six percent, in the rituximab-plus-placebo control arm. Serious and fatal adverse events were higher with the combination, especially in older patients. The stock crashed more than fifty percent to one dollar forty-four. The take from analysts: even with formal endpoint success, that mortality differential will be difficult for physicians, patients, and regulators to ignore. The read for the broader A D C field: payload-driven toxicity is the central limitation, and the difference between a successful confirmatory readout and a commercial disaster can be twenty patient deaths.

Third, Autobahn Therapeutics reported positive phase two topline data for elunetirom in bipolar depression on Wednesday. Elunetirom is an oral, once-daily, brain-penetrant central nervous system thyroid hormone receptor agonist — pursued as adjunctive therapy on top of background mood stabilizers. The company called the antidepressant effect rapid, robust, and durable, and announced plans to advance into phase three. The F D A also granted fast track designation. The mechanism note for Calibr: thyroid hormone receptor agonism is the same receptor family Madrigal's resmetirom hits for MASH — Autobahn has reformulated for brain penetration and central nervous system selectivity rather than the hepatic beta-isoform selectivity that Madrigal exploits. The cross-indication architecture of nuclear receptor targeting continues to surprise.

Fourth, Cabaletta Bio presented updated data at the European Alliance of Associations for Rheumatology meeting in Madrid on Wednesday for rese-cel — its autologous C D nineteen CAR-T cell therapy for autoimmune indications. Across fifty-two evaluable patients spanning myositis, lupus, and systemic sclerosis, eighty-three percent of dermatomyositis patients in the RESET-Myositis study would have met the registrational primary endpoint, with all those patients maintaining response off immunomodulators for as long as a year and a half. The new wrinkle is preconditioning-free lupus dosing — the first two lupus patients dosed without lymphodepleting chemotherapy showed deep B-cell depletion at the lowest dose, on translational signals comparable to patients who received lymphodepletion. This puts the autoimmune CAR-T field in the same place that the in vivo lymphoma CAR-T field sits — chasing the elimination of lymphodepletion as the chief access and toxicity barrier. The competitive parallel to Kelonia, Capstan, and Legend's L B twenty-five-oh-one for oncology is now explicit on the autoimmune side.

Parabilis is a Foresite Capital portfolio company through its January twenty twenty-six three hundred five million dollar series F. The platform — stabilized alpha-helical peptides addressing classically undruggable intracellular targets — is the differentiated science worth watching, particularly for tumor-pathway nodes like beta-catenin where small molecules have repeatedly failed.

Sixth and finally, Barcelona-based Ona Therapeutics closed an oversubscribed eighty-six point six million-dollar series B on Wednesday, co-led by Columbus Venture Partners and Mérieux Equity Partners. Proceeds advance O N A two-fifty-five, a first-in-class antibody-drug conjugate for hormone receptor-positive HER-two-negative breast cancer, into clinical trials, along with O N A three-eighty-nine, a colorectal-cancer ADC. Ona spun out of the Institute for Research in Biomedicine in Barcelona in twenty nineteen. The differentiator the company is leaning on is targeting treatment-resistant tumor biology — the cells that survive standard hormone-receptor-positive HER-two-negative therapy — rather than chasing a more crowded antigen.
