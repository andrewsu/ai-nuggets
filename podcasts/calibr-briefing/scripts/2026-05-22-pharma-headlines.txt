Number one, and this is the biggest readout of the year so far. Eli Lilly announced positive topline data from TRIUMPH dash one, the pivotal Phase three obesity trial for retatrutide — Lilly's first-in-class triple agonist of the G-I-P, G-L-P-one, and glucagon receptors. The trial randomized two-thousand-three-hundred-thirty-nine adults with obesity or overweight and at least one weight-related comorbidity, but without diabetes, across three doses for eighty weeks. Mean weight loss at the four-milligram dose was nineteen percent. At nine milligrams, twenty-five-point-nine percent.
